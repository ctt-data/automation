Ur Brief Introduction to Universal Basic Income

What it is

It is a cash payment.

It is unconditional.

It is periodic.

It is distributed to individuals.

It is distributed to everyone.

What it isn't

not vouchers or in-kind services or goods.

not based on employment status or willingness to work.

not as a one-off grant.

not to households.

not based on existing, independent means or assets.

Pros

  • Reduces poverty and income inequality, and improves health.
  • Leads to positive job growth and lower school dropout rates.
  • Guarantees income for non-working parents and caregivers, thus empowering important unpaid roles, especially for women.
